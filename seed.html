SEED

SEED is a typography recognition app that fosters curiosity and communication around the world.


It looks at the world through the lens of language with an aim to connect people and understand each other better. It showcases the history and vocabulary of the language in a detailed manner.

Challenge
  • With the dearth of communication apps around, users are more than saturated with this sector.

    features that would keep the interaction active.
